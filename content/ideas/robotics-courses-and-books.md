## Hugging Face Robotics Course
Within Hugging Face's [Learn][03], there is a free course, [Robotics Course][01],
that will take you on a journey, from classical robotics to modern learning-based approaches,
in understanding, implementing, and applying machine learning techniques to real robotic systems.
This course is based on the "[Robot Learning: A Tutorial][02]", which is a comprehensive guide to robot learning for researchers and practitioners.
Here, we are attempting to distill the tutorial into a more accessible format for the community.
